## Video & Audio Compression Specs

### MP4 for web (Android + iPhone)
**Target: under 3 MB for 30-60 second clips**

**HandBrake settings (free, Mac/Windows):**
```
Container:  MP4
Codec:      H.264 (x264)
Quality:    RF 23 (constant quality — never use bitrate)
Preset:     Veryfast
Resolution: 1280×720 (720p) — no need for 4K on web
Audio:      AAC 128 kbps, Stereo
```

**FFmpeg command (one-liner, works for any clip):**
```bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 \
  -c:v libx264 -crf 23 -preset veryfast -movflags +faststart \
  -vf scale=1280:720 \
  -c:a aac -b:a 128k \
  output-web.mp4
```
`-movflags +faststart` = video plays while downloading (critical for mobile)

**For hero/background videos (no audio needed):**
```bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 \
  -c:v libx264 -crf 28 -preset veryfast -movflags +faststart \
  -vf scale=1280:720 -an \
  hero-bg.mp4
```
Target size: under 1.5 MB for background loops.

### MP3 / Audio for web
```bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp3 -b:a 128k -ar 44100 output-web.mp3
# For voice-only (podcasts, testimonials):
ffmpeg -i input.mp3 -b:a 96k -ar 44100 output-voice.mp3
```

### HTML video tag (correct implementation)
```html
<video
  width="1280" height="720"
  poster="gestion-velora-preview.jpg"
  preload="none"
  loading="lazy"
  controls>
  <source src="/videos/gestion-velora-montreal.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Votre navigateur ne supporte pas la vidéo HTML5.
</video>

<!-- Background/muted autoplay loop: -->
<video autoplay muted loop playsinline
  width="1280" height="720"
  poster="hero-poster.jpg">
  <source src="/videos/hero-bg.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>
```

**`poster=`** is required — it's the image shown before the video loads (also indexed by Google).

## Backlinks Strategy — How to Get Them

Google weighs backlinks heavily. Here are sources that actually work for a Montreal property management company:

### 1. Local directories (free, do today)
- **Google Business Profile** — google.com/business — add all services, photos, posts
- **Yelp Canada** — yelp.ca
- **Yellow Pages Canada** — yellowpages.ca
- **CanadaOne** — canadaone.com
- **Hotfrog Canada** — hotfrog.ca
- **BBB Montreal** — bbb.org (Better Business Bureau)
- **Chambre de commerce du Montréal métropolitain** — ccmm.ca
- **APCHQ** (Association des professionnels de la construction) — apchq.com
- **CORPIQ** (Corporation des propriétaires immobiliers du Québec) — corpiq.qc.ca
- **RGCQ** (Regroupement des gestionnaires et copropriétaires du Québec) — rgcq.org ← **highest value**

### 2. Content-driven backlinks (2-4 weeks)
Write one article on each of these topics and pitch to Quebec property sites:
- "Loi 141 : ce que chaque syndicat de copropriété doit savoir" → pitch to rgcq.org, blogue notarial, avocat-condo.ca
- "Comment fonctionne un fonds de prévoyance au Québec" → pitch to APCHQ, PME Montréal
- "Airbnb à Montréal : réglementation complète 2024" → pitch to bloggers, forums de copropriété

### 3. Partner pages (reciprocal, local)
Reach out to:
- Notaires spécialisés en copropriété (link exchange: you send clients to them, they list you)
- Courtiers immobiliers sur l'île de Montréal
- Entrepreneurs en bâtiment (they do repairs, you manage — natural partnership)
- Avocats en droit immobilier québécois

### 4. Press / media (medium term)
- Send a press release to **Journal de Montréal** and **La Presse** when you hit a milestone (50 buildings managed, new tech feature, etc.)
- Submit to **Immobilier Commercial** (immobiliercommercial.ca)
- Submit to **Actualités Habitation** (actualiteshabitation.com)
